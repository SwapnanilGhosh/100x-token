WHY HOLD IS GOLD?

The phrase "hold is gold" is often used in the context of cryptocurrency trading and investing. It refers to the practice of holding onto your cryptocurrency assets for an extended period of time instead of selling them during small market fluctuations.


The idea behind this strategy is that cryptocurrency values tend to rise and fall rapidly over short periods, but over a longer period, they generally increase in value. Therefore, by holding onto your cryptocurrencies for a long time, you can potentially realize substantial gains when their values increase.
